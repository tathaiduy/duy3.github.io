HAPPY TET – FUNDRAISING EVENT

In light of this sharing event, 45 children who keep fighting for their dream will be handed a gift set including books, milk and sweets. SPRING VOLUNTARY OPERATION 2020

The old Golden Swallows have flown back from "Warm Summer", only to bring spring news today and remind them of a warm spring. These days, the Golden Swallow has entered the harvest season, working day and night to weave "LOC TAN XUAN".... Luu Nghiep Anh is a poor commune in Tra Cu district, Tra Vinh province, with more than 64% ethnic minorities, life is difficult. The people here live mainly by growing sugarcane, raising fish, working as hired laborers.

Meet children in the Go Cong Dong

On July 18, YCYD members and students from Go Cong Dong district, Tien Giang held an intimate meeting to share with them about YCYD's next plans. Through exciting games, requiring high teamwork, YCYD and the children had moments of relaxation, laughter, creating memorable memories and connecting YCYD with the children more closely. Also in this meeting, a number of important activities were launched by the group such as: teaching English learning online, announcing the StoryTeller contest, and awarding scholarships for the upcoming school year.

START THE DREAM CREATION JOURNEY IN 2020

With the goal of helping and accompanying more students who are studious but have difficult family circumstances, YCYD team will visit and interact with students in Go Cong Dong. In this school year, YCYD increases the total number of students supported to 70 students, which means 42 talented students will be selected at the upcoming meeting. When they are lucky enough to receive support from YCYD, each child will receive a scholarship of 3.5 million VND/year. Although the amount is not much, YCYD hopes to spread more positive values, helping to bring a brighter future to the students.

WELCOME TRIP 25/10 - DISCOVER SAI GON, ENJOY THE NEW SCHOOL YEAR

In the 2019-2020 school year, YCYD has helped more than 40 disadvantaged students in Go Cong Dong district, following in their footsteps to confidently pursue the learning path ahead. The school year 2020 - 2021 promises to be a year of great success as YCYD has just completed recruiting 30 more students, bringing the total number of supported students to 70.

Spring Voluntary

When spring comes, we pray for "Phuoc - Loc - Tho - Hy - Tai" to bring back a great thousand full of cold wind. This spring, the Golden Swallow ... invites each other to bring back Loc Lam commune, Bao Lam district, Lam Dong province a meaningful Golden season with the theme "NGU LOC XUAN".

Timing

Campaign period: From December 20, 2021 to January 9, 2022

Local operating time: From 07/01/2022 to 09/01/2022

Area of operation: Loc Lam commune, Bao Lam district, Lam Dong province.

PROGRAM S series "Warriors Are Here"

Daily local radio activities to introduce the day's activities to help people attend, share and create opportunities for soldiers to be closer to Loc Lam people.

Spring contributes

Soldiers will experience the actual daily work of the people here. In order to create conditions for soldiers to have the opportunity to exchange and live with relatives, to understand more about the nature of work as well as the main occupation of Loc Lam commune.

SCIENCE FESTIVAL

The soldiers will coordinate with Nguyen Van Troi Primary and Secondary School to organize a Science Day consisting of two main parts: the contest "Water rocket" - making water rockets, and the booth "Science Exhibition" study” for junior high school students. Thereby helping children get closer to physics or scientific phenomena, creating excitement and inspiration for them, increasing their curiosity and stimulation when explaining phenomena.

Remember Spring

Soldiers will visit, clean and offer flowers at the Monument to Heroes of Martyrs. This is also a charity activity to remember the gratitude of those who rested in the resilient red land that was once the base of guerilla warfare here.

Cultivation spring

The soldiers will cooperate with the locality to build toilets in the cultural house of village 3 and help households with special difficulties in finding fuel. good for households.

Spring Smile

The target audience of this program is the children of Loc Lam Commune Kindergarten. Accordingly, the soldiers will join hands to build and improve the play space for the children to be more colorful, more joyful and more diverse. Besides, the soldiers will create fun games for the children to play. I join together. This is an opportunity for the soldiers to interact with the children and also an opportunity for the children to have fun during the coming Tet holidays.

ARTS AND GIFTS AWARDING PROGRAM "NGu LOC XUAN"

The music night is a space for the entire people of Loc Lam commune to bond more closely with the Golden Season Soldiers, a meaningful spiritual gift that creates a joyful and bustling New Year atmosphere in the midst of immense thousands. The program of Arts and Gifts "Ngu Loc Xuan" is a night of exchange, gift giving and love exchange between young enthusiastic soldiers and local people. We hope that the music night will be an unforgettable mark for the people of Loc Lam commune as well as the soldiers
